Fat is not the enemy. It’s the body’s most efficient energy source, especially when carbohydrates are removed. This article breaks down why animal fat is crucial for metabolic health, brain function, and hormonal balance.

By switching to fat as fuel, you stabilize energy, reduce cravings, and improve endurance. Ditch the sugar rollercoaster.
