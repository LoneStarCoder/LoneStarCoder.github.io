Start the Carnivore Diet

Getting started is simple: eliminate all plant foods, processed crap, sugars, and grains. Eat only animal-based foods.

  • Beef, lamb, pork, chicken, game meats
  • Organ meats (liver, heart, kidney, etc.)
  • Animal fats (tallow, butter)
  • Salt, water, and electrolytes

Do this for 30 days and watch your body reset. No macros, no calorie counting—just eat when hungry, stop when full.

The Core Protocol

These are the non-negotiables of Carnivore Core:

  • 🥩 Meat is your primary source of nutrition—aim for fatty cuts.
  • 🧂 Salt your food and hydrate well—add electrolytes if needed.
  • ❌ Avoid plants, sugar, seed oils, and all processed foods.
  • 🔥 Eat nose-to-tail when possible for full nutrient density.
  • 🕐 No snacking. Eat real meals based on real hunger.
